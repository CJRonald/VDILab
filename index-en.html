About Us

Visual AI Leading Burn Care, Rebuilding the Future of Burn Center

CGMH Burn Center AI Lab was established in 2025, jointly formed by the Linkou Chang Gung Burn Center, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, and Chang Gung University AI teams. We are dedicated to developing clinical applications that combine visual natural image analysis, natural language processing, multimodal artificial intelligence models, and visual question answering technologies. Our research focuses on automated assessment of burn area and depth, inhalation injury risk prediction, medical record and image integration analysis, and clinical intelligent decision support system development. Our goal is to create practical AI tools that enhance the precision, efficiency, and humanization of burn care.

Research Areas

Our research covers all important aspects of burn medicine

TBSA and Depth AI Automatic Recognition

Utilizing deep learning technology to automatically analyze burn area (TBSA) and depth, providing objective and precise assessment results to assist physicians in developing treatment plans.

Inhalation Injury Risk Prediction Model

Establishing prediction models to assess patient inhalation injury risk, enabling early identification of high-risk patients and preventive treatment to reduce complication rates.

Blood Product and Surgical Outcome Analysis

Analyzing the correlation between blood product usage and surgical outcomes, optimizing transfusion strategies to improve surgical success rates and reduce complications.
