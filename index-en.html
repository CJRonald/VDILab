About VDI Lab

Visual Intelligence for Smarter Intensive Burn Care

CGMH Burn Center Visual-Driven Intelligence Lab (VDI Lab) was established in 2025 by the Linkou CGMH Burn Center, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, and Chang Gung University AI teams. The lab is dedicated to developing clinical applications that combine visual image analysis, natural language processing, multimodal AI models, and visual question answering technologies.

Our research focuses on automated assessment of burn area and depth, inhalation injury risk prediction, integrated analysis of medical records and images, and the development of clinical intelligent decision support systems. Our goal is to create practical AI tools to enhance the precision, efficiency, and humanization of burn care.

Research

Our research covers all important aspects of burn medicine

AI-based TBSA & Depth Recognition

Using deep learning to automatically analyze burn area (TBSA) and depth, providing objective and precise assessment to assist physicians in treatment planning.

Inhalation Injury Risk Prediction

Building models to predict inhalation injury risk, enabling early identification and preventive treatment for high-risk patients, reducing complications.

Blood Product & Surgical Outcome Analysis

Analyzing the relationship between blood product use and surgical outcomes, optimizing transfusion strategies to improve success rates and reduce complications.
